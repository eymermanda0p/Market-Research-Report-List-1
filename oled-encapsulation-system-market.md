<p><ul><li>TFE</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED encapsulation system market primarily consists of two types: TFE (Transparent Functional Encapsulation) and Hybrid encapsulation. TFE utilizes transparent films to protect organic light-emitting diodes from moisture and oxygen, enhancing device longevity. Hybrid encapsulation combines various materials, such as glass and polymers, offering improved durability and flexibility. Both types aim to optimize OLED performance while minimizing degradation from environmental factors, catering to diverse applications in consumer electronics, automotive displays, and lighting solutions.</p></p>
