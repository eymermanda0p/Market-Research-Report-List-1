<p><p>The VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) market is divided into two primary types: single-mode and multimode VCSELs. Single-mode VCSELs focus on a single wavelength and are ideal for long-distance data transmission due to their lower loss and higher bandwidth capabilities. In contrast, multimode VCSELs support multiple wavelengths, making them suitable for short-distance applications like data centers and telecommunications. Each type caters to specific industry needs, influencing their adoption in various optical networking and communication systems.</p></p>
