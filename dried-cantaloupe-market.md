<p><p>The dried cantaloupe market features two primary types: baked dried and freeze-dried. Baked dried cantaloupe is created by dehydrating the fruit using heat, resulting in a chewy texture and concentrated flavor, ideal for snacks and baking. In contrast, freeze-dried cantaloupe undergoes a process that preserves its structure and nutrients by freezing and then removing moisture, leading to a crispy texture. This type retains more of the fruit's original flavor and is often used in health foods and long-term storage.</p></p>
