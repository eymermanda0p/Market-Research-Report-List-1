<p><strong>The Pressure Vacuum Vent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Only</li><li>Vacuum Only</li><li>Combined Pressure/Vacuum Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pressure Vacuum Vent market consists of three primary types: Pressure Only, Vacuum Only, and Combined Pressure/Vacuum Valves. Pressure Only valves are designed to release excess pressure from tanks, protecting them from rupture. Vacuum Only valves allow for the entry of air when internal pressure drops, preventing collapse. Combined Pressure/Vacuum valves serve both functions, regulating pressure and preventing vacuum conditions simultaneously. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining the safety and integrity of storage systems in various industries.</p></p>
