<p><p>The Smoked Atlantic Salmon market is divided into two primary types: hot smoking and cold smoking. Hot smoking involves cooking the salmon at higher temperatures, resulting in a flakier, fully cooked product with a smoky flavor and a moist texture. On the other hand, cold smoking cures the fish without cooking it, preserving its delicate texture and silky taste, while infusing it with a subtle smokiness. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences and culinary applications, expanding market opportunities.</p></p>
