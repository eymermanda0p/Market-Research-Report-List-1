<p><p>The flange spreader market encompasses tools used in maintenance and assembly of flanged connections. Mechanical flange expanders utilize manual force to separate flanges, ideal for smaller, accessible spaces where hydraulic power may be unnecessary. On the other hand, hydraulic flange expanders leverage hydraulic pressure for more powerful and efficient separation, suitable for larger or heavily corroded flanges. Both types enhance safety and ease of operations, reducing the risk of damage during disassembly and maintenance tasks in various industries.</p></p>
