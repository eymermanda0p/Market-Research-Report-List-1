<p><ul><li>Moss Type (Spherical Storage Tank)</li><li>Membrane Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LNG carriers are specialized vessels for transporting liquefied natural gas, categorized mainly into two types: Moss type and Membrane type. Moss type carriers feature spherical storage tanks, known for their robustness and flexibility in design, which helps to minimize the risk of leaks. Membrane type carriers use thin, flexible membranes that are integrated into the ship's hull, offering efficiency and maximizing cargo space. Both types are crucial in the LNG market, catering to different operational requirements and safety standards.</p></p>
