<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pipe Handling Systems</li><li>Electric Pipe Handling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe handling system market comprises two primary types: hydraulic and electric systems. Hydraulic pipe handling systems utilize fluid pressure to lift and maneuver pipes, offering high power and efficiency in heavy-duty applications. They are often preferred for their robustness in harsh environments. Conversely, electric pipe handling systems rely on electric motors and are characterized by their precision and ease of use. While they typically have lower lifting capacities, they are more versatile and energy-efficient, making them suitable for various operational needs.</p></p>
