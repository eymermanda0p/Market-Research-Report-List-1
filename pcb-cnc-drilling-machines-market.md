<p><p>The PCB CNC drilling machines market is segmented into three main types: mechanical drilling, laser drilling, and others. Mechanical drilling employs rotating bits to create precise holes in PCBs, offering speed and cost-effectiveness for mass production. Laser drilling utilizes high-powered lasers to produce micro holes with exceptional accuracy, ideal for fine-pitch components and complex designs. The "others" segment includes emerging technologies and hybrid methods that combine features of both mechanical and laser drilling, catering to specialized applications and requirements.</p></p>
