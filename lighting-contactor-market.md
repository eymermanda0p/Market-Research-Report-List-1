<p><strong>The Lighting Contactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Held</li><li>Mechanically Held</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting contactor market is divided into electrically held and mechanically held types. Electrically held contactors use electromagnetic forces to maintain their position when energized, allowing for remote operation and quick response. Conversely, mechanically held contactors rely on physical latching mechanisms to maintain their state, providing enhanced reliability during power outages. Both types serve to control lighting systems efficiently, with electrically held options offering more flexibility for automation, while mechanically held options are preferred for their stability and simplicity.</p></p>
