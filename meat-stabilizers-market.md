<p><p>The meat stabilizers market includes various types that enhance texture, moisture retention, and flavor in meat products. Gums, such as guar and xanthan, provide viscosity and stability. Pectin, derived from fruits, helps in gel formation and moisture retention. Carrageenan, extracted from seaweed, improves texture and prevents separation. Gelatin, sourced from animal collagen, offers binding properties. Other stabilizers encompass plant-based alternatives and modified starches, catering to diverse consumer preferences and enhancing the overall quality of processed meat products.</p></p>
