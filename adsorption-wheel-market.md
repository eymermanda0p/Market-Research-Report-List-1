<p><p>An adsorption wheel, also known as a desiccant rotary wheel or molecular sieve wheel, is a device designed to remove moisture from the air by utilizing adsorption technology. These wheels are commonly employed in commercial and industrial applications such as HVAC systems, dehumidification processes, and energy recovery ventilation. The mechanism involves rotating a wheel coated with a desiccant material that captures moisture from the air when it passes through, subsequently releasing it during a regeneration phase.</p><p>The Adsorption Wheel Market is on an upward trajectory, fueled by increasing awareness of energy efficiency and the need for effective humidity control solutions across various sectors. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period. Prominent trends include the integration of advanced materials and technologies that enhance efficiency and regeneration processes. Additionally, there is a rising demand for energy-efficient systems in response to environmental regulations and sustainability goals. As industries become more focused on optimizing indoor air quality and reducing energy consumption, the adsorption wheel presents a viable solution, making it an essential component in modern air treatment systems.</p></p>
