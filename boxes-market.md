<p><p>Boxes in the market are categorized based on their corrugation layers: single, double, and triple. Single corrugated boxes consist of one layer of fluted material between two liners, offering basic protection for lightweight items. Double corrugated boxes have two layers of fluting, providing enhanced durability and support for heavier products. Triple corrugated boxes feature three layers of fluting, making them ideal for very heavy or fragile goods, ensuring maximum strength and protection during shipping and storage.</p></p>
