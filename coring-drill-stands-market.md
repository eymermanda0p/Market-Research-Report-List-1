<p><ul><li>Tiltable stands</li><li>Gyro stands</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coring drill stands are essential for geotechnical and mineral exploration drilling, classified mainly into two types: tiltable stands and gyro stands. Tiltable stands allow for adjustable drilling angles, enhancing versatility and precision in various geological conditions. Gyro stands, on the other hand, utilize gyroscopic stabilization to maintain accuracy during drilling, minimizing deviations. Both types cater to specific operational needs, facilitating efficient core sampling while ensuring stability and reducing human error in diverse drilling environments.</p></p>
