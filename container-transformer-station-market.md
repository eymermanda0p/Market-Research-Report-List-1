<p><p>The Container Transformer Station market encompasses three main types based on voltage ratings: Low Voltage, Medium Voltage, and High Voltage. Rated Low Voltage stations are designed for applications under 1 kV, typically used in residential and commercial areas. Rated Medium Voltage stations handle voltages from 1 kV to 36 kV, serving industrial and municipal needs. Rated High Voltage stations operate above 36 kV, crucial for power transmission and large-scale distribution systems. Each type caters to specific infrastructure requirements and operational environments.</p></p>
