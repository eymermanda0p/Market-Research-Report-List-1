<p><strong>The EUV Light Sources Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Energetic Plasma</li><li>Synchrotron Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) light sources market primarily comprises two types: energetic plasma and synchrotron radiation. Energetic plasma sources generate EUV light by using high-energy plasma to emit radiation, making them suitable for semiconductor lithography. Conversely, synchrotron radiation sources produce EUV light through the acceleration of charged particles, allowing for high intensity and tunable wavelengths. Both technologies cater to advanced manufacturing and scientific applications, with energetic plasma being more prevalent in commercial settings while synchrotron radiation is used for research purposes.</p></p>
