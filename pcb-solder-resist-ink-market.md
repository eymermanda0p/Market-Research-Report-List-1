<p><ul><li>Photoimageable SRI</li><li>UV Curable SRI</li><li>Thermal Curable SRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB Solder Resist Ink market is divided into three main types: Photoimageable Solder Resist Ink (SRI), UV Curable SRI, and Thermal Curable SRI. Photoimageable SRI uses photopolymerization to create intricate patterns and offers high precision for fine line applications. UV Curable SRI cures rapidly under ultraviolet light, providing excellent adhesion and durability, making it suitable for high-speed production. Thermal Curable SRI relies on heat for curing, offering robust performance for high-temperature environments and improved chemical resistance.</p></p>
