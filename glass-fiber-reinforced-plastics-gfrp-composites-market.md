<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics (GFRP) composites are classified into two main market types: general-purpose glass fibers and special-purpose glass fibers. General-purpose glass fibers are widely used for various applications due to their cost-effectiveness and versatility, making them suitable for standard industrial products. In contrast, special-purpose glass fibers are engineered for specific applications, offering enhanced properties like increased strength, durability, or chemical resistance, suited for demanding environments such as aerospace, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
