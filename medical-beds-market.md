<p><p>The medical beds market is divided into two primary types: manual and electric medical beds. Manual medical beds require physical effort to adjust positions and height, making them more cost-effective but potentially less convenient for caregivers and patients. In contrast, electric medical beds utilize motors for easy adjustments, enhancing patient comfort and accessibility, particularly for those with mobility issues. These electric beds are generally preferred in hospitals and long-term care facilities due to their user-friendly features and operational efficiency.</p></p>
