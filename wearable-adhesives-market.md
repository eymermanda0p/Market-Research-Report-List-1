<p><p>The wearable adhesives market comprises acrylic-based, silicone-based, and other types. Acrylic-based adhesives are known for their strong bonding and quick-drying properties, making them suitable for various applications. Silicone-based adhesives offer flexibility, biocompatibility, and moisture resistance, ideal for medical and skin-contact devices. The choice of adhesive type depends on factors like intended use, skin sensitivity, environmental conditions, and the required durability of the wearable device.</p></p>
