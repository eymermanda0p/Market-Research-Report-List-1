<p><p>Chromate conversion coating services involve applying a protective layer on metal surfaces, primarily aluminum, to enhance corrosion resistance and paint adhesion. The market can be categorized into two main types: chromates and dichromates. Chromates contain hexavalent chromium and offer excellent corrosion protection but are more hazardous due to toxicity. Dichromates, which are often used as a less toxic alternative, provide similar protective benefits while posing fewer environmental and health risks. Both types are essential in aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
