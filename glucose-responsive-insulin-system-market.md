<p><p>The Glucose Responsive Insulin System market encompasses various insulin types tailored for effective glucose management. Rapid-acting insulins provide quick blood sugar control shortly after administration. Short-acting insulins have a slightly delayed onset but remain effective for a few hours. Intermediate-acting insulins offer prolonged effects, useful for daily glucose regulation. Long-acting insulins ensure stable, gradual absorption for 24 hours or more. Premixed types combine rapid and intermediate insulins for convenience, allowing for flexible dosing strategies throughout the day.</p></p>
