<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle tire market primarily consists of two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature steel belts that run perpendicular to the tire's direction, offering improved traction, fuel efficiency, and longevity, making them popular for passenger vehicles. In contrast, bias tires have layers that crisscross, providing a more flexible structure but typically lower performance in terms of durability and heat dissipation. Bias tires are often used in agricultural and industrial applications due to their robustness.</p></p>
