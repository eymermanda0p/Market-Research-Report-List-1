<p><ul><li>Loop Powered</li><li>Independent Powered</li><li>Output Loop Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isolators are critical components in industrial settings, designed to prevent interference and enhance safety in signal transmission. The loop-powered isolators derive power from the signal loop, ensuring minimal installation complexity and reduced wiring requirements. Independent powered isolators utilize an external power source, allowing for greater flexibility in design and operation. Output loop-powered isolators receive power from the output signal loop, ensuring compatibility with various devices while maintaining signal integrity and providing essential isolation for sensitive components.</p></p>
