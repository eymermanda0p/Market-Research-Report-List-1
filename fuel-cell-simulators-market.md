<p><p>Fuel cell simulators are essential tools for testing and developing fuel cell technologies. The market is divided into two primary types: single-channel and multi-channel simulators. Single-channel simulators focus on individual fuel cell stack performance, providing precise data for specific configurations. In contrast, multi-channel simulators can test multiple stacks simultaneously, allowing for more complex system analysis and efficiency evaluations. This diversity enables researchers and manufacturers to optimize fuel cell designs and applications across varying scales and functionalities.</p></p>
