<p><strong>The Wireless Remote Control Vehicle Barrier Gate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless remote control vehicle barrier gate market includes two main types: straight and crank barriers. Straight barriers feature a single, horizontal arm that opens and closes vertically, allowing for straightforward vehicle access, typically used in commercial and residential settings. Crank barriers, on the other hand, utilize a manual crank mechanism to raise and lower the arm, offering a more customizable method for controlling access. Both types enhance security and vehicle management in parking lots, toll booths, and restricted areas.</p></p>
