<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Smart Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Tags</li><li>Active Tags</li><li>Battery Assisted Passive Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID smart labels market comprises three primary types: passive tags, active tags, and battery-assisted passive tags. Passive tags rely on external readers for power and communication, making them cost-effective for large-scale applications like inventory management. Active tags have their own power source, enabling longer read ranges and additional features, suitable for tracking high-value assets. Battery-assisted passive tags combine elements of both, using a battery to boost performance while still being relatively inexpensive, ideal for versatile uses in logistics and supply chain management.</p></p>
