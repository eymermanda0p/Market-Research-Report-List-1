<p><strong>The Analytical Electron Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Electron Microscope</li><li>Scanning Electron Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Analytical Electron Microscope market comprises two primary types: Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) and Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM). TEM utilizes a focused beam of electrons transmitted through a specimen, providing high-resolution images and detailed internal structure analysis at the atomic level. In contrast, SEM scans the surface of a sample with a focused electron beam, producing three-dimensional images and surface morphology insights. Both types are essential in materials science, biology, and nanotechnology research for their unique imaging capabilities.</p></p>
